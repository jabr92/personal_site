Personal

I usually introduce myself with an explanation: "John Arin. Both parts are my first name." I was born and raised in Redding, CA then moved to the San Francisco Bay Area, where I still live, for college. My academic interests include the physical sciences, especially chemistry, and computers/technology. In addition to gaining and applying technical knowledge, I find satisfaction in exploring wilderness areas and preparing meals for those I love.

Experience

My first job was with the Community Service Officer (CSO) Program of UCPD Berkeley. I helped ensure the safety of the UC Berkeley campus community starting with my boots on the ground and working my way up to serving as Administrative Program Coordinator for the final two and a half of my more than five years with the program.

During my time at UC Berkeley I also worked in the Thermal Hydraulics laboratory of the Department of Nuclear Engineering. The goal of my project was to validate the emergency shutdown system for a next-generation nuclear reactor.

Currently I work at Hurricane Electric, an internet company which operates a global backbone network and hosts servers in Silicon Valley. My main responsibility is diagnosing and resolving customers' technical issues, but I spend the majority of my time programming. Every day I learn new things about how computers and the internet work.

For examples of the prgramming projects I have done, check out my cool stuff page.

Education

I spent my senior year of high school on the campus of my local community college, where I took courses in calculus, physics, and computer science.
